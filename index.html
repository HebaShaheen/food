Having a healthy life with vegetables,fruits and meat

Fruit and vegetables should be an important part every day. They are naturally good and contain vitamins and minerals that can help to keep you healthy. They can also help protect against some diseases. active lifestyle. There are many varieties of fruit and vegetables available and many ways to prepare, cook and serve them. You should eat at least five serves of vegetables and two serves of fruit each day. Choose different colours and varieties. Fruits and vegetables are low in fat, salt and sugar. They are a good source of dietary fibre. As part of a balanced food, active lifestyle, a high intake of fruit and vegetables can help you to:

  • Reduce obesity and maintain a healthy weight
  • Lower your cholesterol
  • Lower your blood pressure
  • Help Your Skin
  • Reduce the Risk of Chronic Disease

what is the effects of greasy food on your body

Most foods that are fried or cooked with excess oils are considered greasy. They include french fries, potato chips, onion rings, cheeseburgers, and doughnuts. These items have a high calories, fat, salt, and refined carbs but low in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Greasy foods negatively affect your body and health in both the short and long term.

  • cause bloating, stomach pain, and diarrhea
  • lead to weight gain and obesity
  • raise your risk of diabetes
  • increase your risk of heart disease and stroke
  • impair brain function
  • May cause acne
